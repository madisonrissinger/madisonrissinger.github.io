Best Bars in Dauphin County

Check out some of the most visited and highly rated bars in the area!

The Chocolate Avenue Grille

https://www.chocolateavenuegrill.com/

(717)835-0888
114 Chocolate Ave, Hershey, PA 17033

Chocolate Avenue Grille is a Hershey local spot for good drinks and food. The menu has a wide variety to satisfy every customer. Tourists, regulars, and all other hungry people in the area will visit this spot in downtown Hershey for the oudoor seating and superb service.

The Brick Haus


(717)233-4287
229 N 2nd St, Harrisburg, PA 17101

The Brick Haus is a night-time bar with limited food options. It is popular for locals that live in downtown Harrisburg as well as service industry people that get off later in the night. The atmosphere and activities are the seller, the activties on their upper-floor such as the lotto machines and dart boards.

McGrath's Pub

http://mcgrathspub.net/

(717)232-9914
202 Locust St, Harrisburg, PA 17101

Mcgrath's Pub is a two-story Irish restaurant and bar. It has a rustic and authentic atmosphere for people who enjoy a calmer atmophere compared to other bars within the area.


Rubber Soul Brewing Company

https://www.rubbersoulbrewing.com/

(717)220-1741
136 S Hanover St, Hummelstown, Pa 17036

Rubber Soul Brewing Company is a newer restaurant in Hummelstown, PA that offers a bar and restaurant, family-friendly feel. They cater for all different kind of parties while also have a intriguing selection of cocktails and beers on tap that are brewed in-house.

The Millworks

https://millworksharrisburg.com/

(717)695-4888
340 Verbeke St, Harrisburg, PA 17102

The Millworks is a Midtown hotspot for regulars and locals of the Harrisburg area. Tourists within the Harriburg area also will visit this higher-end restaurant with a fine selection of cocktails and tap beers that they brew in house. The food consists of healthier, decent size dishes.

Cafe Fresco

http://www.cafefresco.com/

(717)236-2599
215 N 2nd St, Harrisburg, PA 17101

Cafe Fresco is a higher-end restaurant with a small bar. Reservations are typically reccomended for a table. The food changes regularly with some staples to their menu. Their cocktails are also highly raved about.
